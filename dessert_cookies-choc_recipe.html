Awesome Chocolate Cookies

You need something sweet? Just right now? No problem:
Taste the awesome chocolate cookies, salty and sweet, simple & quick. You will love them.

Ingredients (6-9 cookies)

  • 60-80 g sugar
  • 60 g (neutral tasting) oil
  • 3 tablespoon of soy milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (or sugar vanilla)
  • 120 g flour - spelt or wheat
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  • Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Preheat the oven to 180° C (360° F).
  • Mix sugar, oil, milk, vanilla extract and stir until foamy for 2 minutes. Then add all the other ingredients.
    Stir until you are having a smooth dough.
  • Use hands or a tablespoon to drop the dough on the baking tray and flatten the heaps.
    Bake in the oven for 8 - 10 minutes (depending on the size of the cookies).
    The ideal moment to remove the cookies from the oven is, when they get little fissures on the surface.
  • Let the cookies cool for 5 minutes on the tray.
    If you are patient enough transfer them to a cooling rack and let them cool completely...
  • ... or eat them right away...
  • Note: For beautiful round cookies drop/flatten the dough on the tray in a round shape.
