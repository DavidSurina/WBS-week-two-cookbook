Pizza Mediterana

A simple, but tasty pizza creation. Use southern European ingredients to prepare this spicy dinner adventure!

Ingredients

  • 500 g wheat flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp of yeast
  • 500 ml tomato sauce
  • 200g of grated cheese
  • 1 cup of black olives
  • 1 cup of black olives
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 red onion

Instructions

  • Mix and stir the flour with the water, salt and yeast and leave it for 30 min
  • Slice the red pepper and the onion
  • Spread the dough on a baking tray and also spread the tomato sauce on the dough.
  • Put all over the pizza the slices of red pepper and onion. After it spread the olives as well.
  • Cover it all with the grated cheese as a topping.
  • Put the pizza into the oven (200° Celsius) for 20 min.
  • Take the pizza out it is ready to be served. Caution it is hot!
